HI IM SEAN

Born in LA, raised in OC, bound for NC.


I'm a web developer driven by a thirst for knowledge and a passion for growth.

I've been cruisin' down this web development highway for a solid 25 years. back when I was just a wide-eyed 12-year-old, I stumbled upon the wild world of graphic design. By the time I hit 13, I was cookin' up websites. I quickly sharpened my skills, mastering the Adobe suite along the way. My creative talents found expression in designing eye-catching fliers for local bands and music venues, creating captivating album and merchandise artwork, and developing engaging websites for various musical talents.

So, what did I do? I took a leap, signed up for some courses on EdX and freecodecamp, and embarked on a journey to find my true self. I have since collected six professional certificates, all of which are related to web development, each a testament to my mastery of the ever-shifting tides of web development.


Now, here I am, ready to keep ridin' this wave. I got a head full of wisdom and a creative spark that's hotter than a Los Angeles night. Let's link up and bring innovative web solutions to life.
